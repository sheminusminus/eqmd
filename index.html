For Clients

American Medical Association (AMA) guidelines do not preclude office dispensing as long as state and federal regulatory requirements are fulfilled, the doctor prescribes only to his/her patients and allows them to fill prescriptions where they want.

The AMA Code of Medical Ethics acknowledges that physicians may dispense drugs in their office practices only if such dispensing primarily benefits the patient. Physicians must respect the patient’s freedom to choose where to fill prescriptions. EQMD does not have or exercise any control or direction over physician’s exercise of professional judgment. Information contained herein shall not be construed as medical, legal or other professional advice or opinion.  EQMD does not make any representation or warranty or incur any obligation hereunder.
